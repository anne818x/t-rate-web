About T-rate

Welcome to T-rate, here at T-rate we want you to give your honest opinions about Stenden Universities teachers.

T-rate lets you anonymously rate Stenden teachers. You can also post comments, and see the public comments posted by others. This site is also a useful scorecard, to find out what students are really thinking!
This site is also helpful for the teachers too, they may not be able to login but they can read the reviews to learn what they need to improve upon or learn what they're doing well with.

Login/sign-up, find your teacher(s) and write a review giving your opinion about them.

How T-rate works

Leaving a review
When creating a review for a teacher, you will rate the teacher in five categories. Each category is rated out of five stars. When you have rated the teacher in each category, you then leave a comment about the teacher. This is where you will write your own opinion of this teacher.
T-rate doesn't tolerate profanity, please refain from using it. There is a check in place if you do use any profanity words.
You may only leave a review once per module, this restricts the user from spamming and making a genuine review.

Reporting/ Deleting
If you see a review that you believe is not fair, improper comment or spamming, it is possible to report that review. Once the review has been reported, an admin will look at this and decide whether it should be removed or not.
When you log in you will get automatically directed to the user profile page. Once on there you will see all your reviews that you have left. On this page you are allowed to delete your own review, as an option.

Teacher not listed?
If your teacher is not yet listed, you can add a new teacher and a request shall be sent.

How to help your favourite teacher

Do you want to get your favourite teacher on the Hall of Fame?

The Hall of Fame is what all Stenden teachers are aiming for. They want to show that they're the best at Stenden University. To do that they need you to review them, on T-rate. Each new review will count towards the overall score of each teacher BUT you may only make one review per module. It is all up to the teacher to the reviews up, to become the Top-rated teacher of that module. There are six categories in which Stenden teachers could win an award, they're:
       • Best Atmosphere,
       • Best Professional,
       • Most Helpfulness,
       • Best Lectures,
       • Best Preparation
       • and the one all of the teachers want the Top-rated teacher.

At the end of each module it will be generated who is the best out of each catergory and each teacher will get a badge shown on their page. Another thing that the teacher will recieve is a coupon to get a free cookie.

Make sure that you review your favourite teacher to get them one step closer to that cookie.
